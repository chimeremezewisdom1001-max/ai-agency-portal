How to Pay with OPay / PalmPay:
  1. Open your OPay or PalmPay App.
  2. Tap Transfer -> To Bank.
  3. Look at our secure checkout screen for the generated Bank Name and Account Number.
  4. Paste that account number, select the Bank Name, and transfer.
How to Pay with Your Bank App:
  1. Log into your standard mobile bank app (GTB, Access, Zenith, etc.).
  2. Go to Transfer.
  3. Copy the Wema/Sterling account details from our secure checkout.
  4. Complete the transfer and save your receipt screenshot.
