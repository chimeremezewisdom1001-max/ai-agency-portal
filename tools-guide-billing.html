🇳🇬 Users in Nigeria:

Open the EasyPay Wizard. You can transfer directly to our displayed OPay or Sterling Bank accounts, or use Flutterwave to complete bank/card transfers securely.

🌎 Users in US, Canada, Europe, & UK:

Open the EasyPay Wizard, click "Global Checkout", and pay securely on our verified checkout portal using your international credit/debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or direct local bank links.

If you prefer alternative systems, you can copy our public TRC-20 USDT address, or purchase a Steam, iTunes, or Amazon gift card and submit the 16-digit PIN code directly into our EasyPay Claim Form.
