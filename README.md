# 🤖 Wisdom AI Customer Support & Lead Capture Portal

Welcome to your proprietary AI Agency Portal. This lightweight, high-performance Node.js platform allows agencies to deploy floating AI customer support widgets across client websites, parse custom business guidelines from a central database, and capture booking leads instantly.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express
* **Database:** PostgreSQL (Supabase)
* **AI Engine:** Google Gemini 2.5 Flash API
