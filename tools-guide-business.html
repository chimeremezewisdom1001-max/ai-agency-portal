Business Tools Guide

💼 Why Use Our Business Suite?

Renting warehouse stock optimization systems, purchasing invoice programs, or hiring financial analysts to calculate business valuations costs over ₦25,000+ yearly. Our platform provides professional-grade business metrics completely offline and in-browser to save you money.

📈 What Each Tool Does & How to Use It:

  • Dynamic Cash Book Ledger: Post daily sales and expense records to calculate your true business profit margins locally without using paper ledgers.
  • PDF Receipt & Invoice Creator: Input client details, quantity, and rate to instantly generate a clean transaction receipt with 7.5% standard Nigerian VAT calculated automatically.
  • EOQ Inventory Optimizer: Input annual demand, order placement costs, and annual storage costs to calculate the optimal order size, preventing your money from being locked up in excess dead-stock.
  • Cash Runway Forecaster: Input your starting cash, fixed overhead, and variables to project exactly how many months of operational runway your business has left.
  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Valuation: Project 5-year cash flows and terminal value exit multiples to calculate the data-backed market valuation of your company.
